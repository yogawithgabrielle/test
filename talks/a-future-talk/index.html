A Future Talk

Presented January 1, 2025 at FutureConf 2025

Slides

Story’s talks pages can highlight upcoming appearances, promoting your talks before you present. This is a dummy page illustrating this capability.

You’ll have to add buildFuture: true to your site’s config.yaml to enable this setting. Talks pages that are in the future won’t appear on the homepage or in your RSS feeds; they’ll only appear in the /talks/ list page of your site.

This page also illustrates that you can use PDF slides. Story uses PDF.js to embed a slide viewer. So you don’t have to build slides in RemarkJS, you can host them yourself, like Slideshare (but better).
